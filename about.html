Projects About

Yue Ran was born in 1995 in Chongqing, China. She graduated with a bachelor's degree from Sophia University in Japan and obtained a master's degree in screenwriting and film production at the Catholic University of the Sacred Heart in Milan. Yue has extensive experience in screenwriting for female-centric films and has written and directed several short films.

With cultural backgrounds from China, Japan, France, and Italy, she has developed a strong ability to tell cross-cultural female narratives. She is currently active as a director and editor in Paris and Zurich.
