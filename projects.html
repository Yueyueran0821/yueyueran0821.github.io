Sun, Storm, a Little Girl

China, 80’, Documentary, 2024
Role: Editor · Director: Erni

Erni, a girl from Chengdu, embarked on a three-year sailing adventure with theater actors from across Europe. Along the way, she documented their performances of The Odyssey while reflecting on her own reasons for leaving her hometown in her youth. As the journey unfolded, she encountered diverse migrants and drifters, and through these experiences, she gradually opened her heart, embarking on a path of self-reconciliation.
