Le Passeur

(The Ferryman)
Switzerland, 14’, 2023, Fiction
Writer / Director / Editor
Everything is changing in Alice’s life — a new college, a new boyfriend. To escape from the chaos of her daily routine, Alice retreats to the river, where she meets a young boy: her first love, who died five years ago. As the ferryman returns to guide him to the other side, neither Alice nor the boy are ready to say goodbye. Their delicate bond, suspended between life and memory, begins to shift as the years pass.
